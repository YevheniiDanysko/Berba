Biography

Dimitar Ivanov Berbatov (Bulgarian: Димитър Иванов Бербатов; born 30 January 1981)[3] is a Bulgarian former professional footballer. A striker, he captained the Bulgaria national team from 2006 to 2010, and is the country's all-time leading goalscorer. Renowned for his technique and ball control, he is regarded as one of the greatest Bulgarian players of all time.[5]
